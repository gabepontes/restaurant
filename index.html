Our Story

Brasil Bistro: Where Brazil Comes Alive

Welcome to Brasil Bistro, the place where Brazil comes to life through the art of culinary excellence. Nestled in the heart of California, we are dedicated to bringing the vibrant spirit and authentic flavors of Brazil to your table. With each dish, we strive to encapsulate the essence of Brazilian culture, showcasing its rich heritage, warmth, and love for food.

Popular Dish

Sirloin Cap (Picanha)

Indulge in the mouthwatering flavors of our Sirloin Cap (Picanha) Feast. Grilled to perfection, this Brazilian delicacy will captivate your taste buds. Served with a delightful array of accompaniments, including beans, fluffy rice, zesty vinaigrette, crispy fries, fresh salad, and the traditional Farofa - a savory toasted cassava flour that adds a unique touch to the dish. Immerse yourself in the authentic tastes of Brazil with every delectable bite!
